Introductions#

Hi! I’m Michael a first year Computer Science student at the University of Warwick and this is my first attempt at creating a personal website and blog!

The Future#

I hope to use this blog as a platform for the place that is my messy brain. Whether that be articles explaining something useful, random ramblings about life or sharing work I have completed. It won’t be very structured, but it will be… me!
